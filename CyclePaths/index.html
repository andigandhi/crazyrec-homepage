Cycle Paths was formed in 2016 - since then the Band has started to write their own songs.
After a few lineup changes till 2018 they go strong as a four piece and released their first Record „Teleportation Station“ on the label Crazy Records. This gave them the opportunity to share stages with Bands like BadCopBadCop, Not Available and Bundles.

Due to the pandemic they couldn't play any shows and decided to focus on the songwriting and hit the studio again in 2021/22. The result is a new 13 songs Record which is set to release in spring 2023. Currently the band is working on music videos and planning things for the release.
